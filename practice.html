Tagore society


Welcome to the Tagore Society, where we celebrate the life and works of Rabindranath Tagore, the first non-European Nobel laureate in Literature. Join us in exploring his poetry, music, and philosophy that continue to inspire generations.



library


Our library houses a vast collection of Tagore's works, including his poetry, essays, and plays. It is a treasure trove for scholars and enthusiasts alike.
